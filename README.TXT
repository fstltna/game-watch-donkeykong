                                 HOW TO PLAY
                                 -----------
*Click on the front picture to open the game
*Hold "Time" button to view alarm time (set to 12:00 PM)
 Press and release "Time" button to view current time
*Hold "Game A" or "Game B" to view highscores for games A and B
 Press and release "Game A" (easy) or "Game B" (hard) buttons to play
*Instructions:
 - Donkey Kong captured a beautiful girl and carried her into a building
   under construction
 - the brave carpenter, Mario comes to rescue her following them over the
   girders 
 - Donkey Kong throws barrels at Mario to stop him
 - jump barrels while climbing the building
 - do not jump when Mario has any iron bars overhead
 - move the lever on the upper girder to lift the crane
 - jump to get the hook and cut the wire holding the girder on which Donkey
   Kong is standing
 - knock the girder out from under Donkey Kong to save the girl
*Score: 1 point per barrel leaped over on the lower girder
        2 points per barrel leaped over on the middle girder
        20 to 5 points are earned as bonus at every time a wire is cut
        20 points are earned when all the supporting wires are cut and Donkey
        Kong falls
*At 300 points fanfare sounds and one Mario is restored to bullpen; if there
 are NO MISSES when 300 points are reached game goes into "Chance-Time": score
 flashes and all points are awarded at double value until MISS is made
*Maximum display score is 999 points
*Highest scores are saved in the System Registry
*Time function displays automatically about five minutes after game ends



                                   CONTROLS
                                   --------
A) by MOUSE
   Click on the front picture to open the game
   Right-click anywhere to activate the popup menu
   TIME: hold to view alarm time (set to 12:00 PM)
         press to view current time
   GAME A/B: hold to view highscores for games A and B
             press to play game A (easy) or game B (hard)
   ACL: press to reset game
B) by KEYBOARD
   Left/right arrow keys move Mario left/right
   Up/down arrow keys    make Mario climb/descend a ladder
   CONTROL key           makes Mario jump
   ESC                   quits program
